Tucked away in the creative corner of Lansdowne Mews in central Hove, Pocket Audio is an independent recording & production studio based in Hove, focusing on crafting bespoke, high quality sound for our clients at affordable rates.

We seek to cater to the needs of independent bands, musicians and artists, professionally sound engineering their music and helping them to attain their creative visions.

Who are we

Fred Hills is a professional drummer, composer and collaborator based in Hove. Having recently worked as musical director for an award-winning theatre show, Fred’s aims to bring his creative expertise and colourful playing to the various artistic endeavours sought at Pocket Audio Productions.

www.fredhillsmusic.com

Alex Barron is a freelance producer, engineer, mixer and multi-instrumentalist. With over 15-years experience of both playing in and recording/producing bands, Alex’s attention to detail in sound is unparalleled, and will go above and beyond to achieve the best possible result for the clients that he works with.
